La' Casa

La’casa is an affordable Pan African Real Estate Developer focused on sustainable housing in Africa. It is a sister company to Building Survey. Its headquarters are in Kampala, Uganda. La’casa was established in 2017, by a group of social investors in order to put to reality low-income housing models in Africa.The vision of La’casa is to enable ordinary Africans, most of whom are active in the informal sector, to acquire a house within their financial means and raise their families in a safe, clean and healthy environment. In so doing, La’casa achieves social impact, environmental and financial returns.
